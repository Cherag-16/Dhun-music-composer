# Dhun Composition Guide

## How to Create Your First Dhun

### Step 1: Navigate to Compose Page
Click "Compose" in the main navigation menu to access the composition interface.

### Step 2: Enter Your Lyrics
- In the **Lyrics** field, enter or paste your Hindi lyrics
- The system will analyze your lyrics to detect mood and emotion
- Example: "Teri khushboo mein basa hai tera pyaar, duniya bhool jaun main saari"

### Step 3: Select or Auto-Detect Raag
- **Raag Selection**: Choose a classical Indian raag that matches your lyrics mood
  - **Bhairav**: Morning, spiritual, devotional
  - **Yaman**: Evening, romantic, love songs
  - **Kafi**: Melancholic, emotional depth
  - **Bhopali**: Joy, celebrations, happiness
  - **Marwa**: Mystical, spiritual journey
  - **Ahir Bhairav**: Deep emotions, introspection

- If unsure, let the system auto-suggest based on your lyrics (checkbox option)

### Step 4: Choose Your Instrument
Select one of the classical Indian instruments:
- **Harmonium**: Warm, melodic, traditional Bollywood sound
- **Sitar**: Bright, complex, sophisticated classical sound
- **Sarangi**: Soulful, human voice-like, emotional
- **Bansuri**: Flute-like, pure, spiritual
- **Violin**: Crisp, modern classical blend

### Step 5: Optional Settings
- **Title**: Give your composition a name (auto-generated if left blank)
- **Category**: Choose from Bhajan, Romantic, Patriotic, Gazal, Sad Song
- **Tempo**: Set the BPM (60-200, default 120)
- **Auto-Detect Mood**: Let AI analyze your lyrics for mood

### Step 6: Click "Compose Dhun"
The system will:
1. Analyze your lyrics for emotional content
2. Generate a melody respecting raag rules (aroha, avaroha, vadi, samvadi)
3. Create a beautiful tune matching the selected instrument
4. Display the composition within 5-10 seconds

### Step 7: Play and Listen
- Click the **Play Dhun** button to hear your composition
- Adjust volume with the slider
- Progress bar shows playback position
- The melody visualization shows all notes used

### Step 8: Export Your Composition
- Click **Export Composition** to download as WAV file
- Save to your device for sharing or further editing

## Understanding Your Composition

### Composition Info Card
Shows:
- **Title**: Name of your dhun
- **Raag**: Classical raag used
- **Mood**: Detected or selected mood
- **Category**: Song category
- **Instrument**: Sound used for playback
- **Duration**: Total playback time
- **Lyrics**: Your original lyrics

### Melody Visualization
- Shows all notes in your composition as a line graph
- Each blue dot represents a note
- Height indicates pitch (frequency)
- Shows frequency range in Hz

### Audio Player
- **Play/Pause**: Control playback
- **Progress Bar**: See your position in the melody
- **Volume Control**: Adjust volume 0-100%
- **Note Count & Duration**: Quick composition info

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use Meaningful Lyrics**: Longer, more expressive lyrics generate better melodies
2. **Match Raag to Mood**: Romantic lyrics with Yaman raag works best
3. **Consider the Context**: Morning lyrics with Bhairav, celebrations with Bhopali
4. **Try Different Instruments**: Same lyrics sound different with each instrument
5. **Experiment**: Create multiple versions to find your favorite

## Troubleshooting

### Audio Not Playing
- Check browser audio permissions
- Try enabling "Auto-Detect Mood" for better melodic generation
- Refresh the page and try again

### No Composition Generated
- Ensure lyrics field has at least 3-4 words
- Try a different raag-instrument combination
- Clear browser cache and reload

### Visualization Not Showing
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure melody has at least 4 notes
- Try a different browser
